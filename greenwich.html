Project: Advertising campaign (OOH) and branding for the new residential complex "Greenwich" from Golden House Situation: There are a lot of residential complexes on the developer market of Uzbekistan, almost all of them are similar to each other in visual style and presentation. Solution: The branding of the project was embodied in the style of the British quarter with its own heraldry and elegant European style. Also, together with the client, a unique offer for the consumer market was developed - an interest-free installment plan for 5 years. In the outdoor area, we focused on the benefits of the offer and corporate identity. Result: During a three-month advertising campaign, up to 300 people a day visited the Golden House office.
